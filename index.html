Understanding OEB Levels
OEB
1
Low Potency
> 1000 µg/m³ — Standard booths
OEB
2
Moderate Potency
100–1000 µg/m³ — Downflow Booth
OEB
3
High Potency
10–100 µg/m³ — Contained Booth
OEB
4
Very High Potency
1–10 µg/m³ — Isolator Required
OEB
5
Ultra High Potency
< 1 µg/m³ — Glove Box Isolator
